This glossary consolidates the acronyms, norms, and cataloging codes used in the documents, memorials, and records of the **Women Who Weave the Forest project (Consórcio UnB/UFRR/UFAC)**. It is a *living document* that must be updated with each newly added acronym to ensure semantic accuracy and facilitate technical documentation, auditing, and *advocacy*.

---

## 1. Technical Standards, Testing, and Engineering
*   **[ABNT](https://www.abnt.org.br/)**: Brazilian Association of Technical Standards.
*   **ACV**: Lifecycle Assessment.
*   **[ASTM](https://www.astm.org/)**: *American Society for Testing and Materials* (International standards for testing materials and chemical/mechanical properties).
*   **[ISO](https://www.iso.org/)**: *International Organization for Standardization* (International standards for safety and operation, e.g., ISO 16852 for flame arresters).
*   **NB**: Brazilian Norm (Terminology/Historical codex for national standards).
*   **NBR**: Brazilian Reference Standard (Current ABNT certification standard applicable to civil, thermal, mechanical and materials engineering).
*   **TRL**: *Technology Readiness Level* (Scale from 1 to 9).

## 2. Socio-environmental Norms, Regulations, and Certifications
*   **[CAR](https://www.car.gov.br/)**: Rural Environmental Registry.
*   **CCA**: Chromated Copper Arsenate (Toxic phytosanitary treatment. Its use is categorically repelled and mitigated in the project in favor of non-toxic bio-solutions).
*   **[CCB](https://verra.org/programs/ccb-program/)**: *Climate, Community & Biodiversity* (Gold standard of certification and verification for carbon projects with social co-benefits).
*   **[CONAMA](https://www.gov.br/mma/pt-br/assuntos/conselho-nacional-do-meio-ambiente)**: National Environment Council.
*   **CPLI**: Free, Prior and Informed Consent (Mandatory consultation protocol for indigenous peoples and traditional communities).
*   **GEE**: Greenhouse Gases (GHG).
*   **MRV**: *Monitoring, Reporting, and Verification* (Continuous evaluation of carbon projects, reforestation, and regenerative impact).
*   **[ODS](https://brasil.un.org/pt-br/sdgs)**: Sustainable Development Goals (UN Agenda 2030).
*   **PNMCB**: National Policy for Bamboo Management and Cultivation (Bamboo Law - Law No. 12.484/2011).
*   **[PNRS](https://sinir.gov.br/pmrs/)**: National Solid Waste Policy.
*   **[REDD / REDD+](https://redd.mma.gov.br/)**: Reducing Emissions from Deforestation and forest Degradation (+ conservation, sustainable management, and enhancement of carbon stocks).
*   **[Cancun Safeguards](https://redd.mma.gov.br/pt/salvaguardas)**: Guidelines defined by the UNFCCC for REDD+ projects to prevent environmental risks and protect the knowledge and lands of local communities.
*   **[VCS](https://verra.org/programs/verified-carbon-standard/)**: *Verified Carbon Standard* (Primary certification standard of the voluntary carbon market by Verra).
*   **[VM0044](https://verra.org/methodologies/vm0044-methodology-for-biochar-utilization-in-soil-and-non-soil-applications/)**: Formal Verra methodology for accounting for emission reductions from Biochar use (*Biochar Utilization in Soil and Non-Soil Applications*).

## 3. Codes and Technologies of the Institucional Platform
*   **[ATHIS](../01_TECNOLOGIA_Institucional/TAK_relatorio-executivo-v1.0.1.md)**: Technical Assistance for Social Housing.
*   **[AVT](../.agents/rules/GOV_regimento-pvit-audiovisual.md)**: Institucional Audiovisual (Reference to video/image-curated production and instructional processes).
*   **[BER](../../04_GESTAO_OPERACIONAL/01_FLUXO_TECNICO/INVENCOES/T02_REATOR_BIORREFINARIA/TAK_biorrefinaria-reuso-comunitaria.md)**: Riverside Ecological Toilet.
*   **[BIOCHAR](../02_PESQUISA_DESENVOLVIMENTO/TAK_nota-tecnica-saneamento-ecologico.md)**: Biochar (Stabilized charcoal, carbon sink in soil or biocomposite matrices).
*   **[BRF](../08_PROJETOS_ENGENHARIA/T02_BIORREFINARIA/TAK_biorrefinaria-t02-memorial.md)**: Thermal cascading biorefinery for treating Bamboo and natural fibers. Vaporization, drying, conditioning, and others.
*   **[BRFA](../08_PROJETOS_ENGENHARIA/T02_BIORREFINARIA/TAK_biorrefinaria-t02-memorial.md)**: Biorefinery adapted for food processing. Dehydration, vaporization/sterilization, conditioning, and others.
*   **[BSM](../02_PESQUISA_DESENVOLVIMENTO/TAK_nota-tecnica-saneamento-ecologico.md)**: Modular Dry Toilet.
*   **[CAPEX](../01_GOVERNANCA/TAK_orcamento-maquinario-bom.md)**: *Capital Expenditure* (Gross infrastructure and machinery acquisition costs).
*   **[GOV](../00_GOVERNANCA/GOV_PROTOCOLO_5.1.md)**: Indexing code or prefix for the Governance Collection documents.
*   **[HIS](../01_TECNOLOGIA_Institucional/TAK_relatorio-executivo-v1.0.1.md)**: Social Housing.
*   **[MAMONEX](../01_TECNOLOGIA_Institucional/TAK_memorial-sistema-construtivo-v7.md)**: Non-toxic Castor Oil-based Polyurethane Resin (e.g., Mamonex RD70 / Imperveg), used in biological welding and waterproofing.
*   **[OPEX](../01_GOVERNANCA/TAK_parametros-mercado-norte.md)**: *Operational Expenditure* (Ongoing operational costs and expenses).
*   **[OSB](../01_TECNOLOGIA_Institucional/TAK_memorial-sistema-construtivo-v7.md)**: *Oriented Strand Board* (Modular biocomposite boards/panels for siding and structure).
*   **[PI](../01_GOVERNANCA/TAK_memorial-contrapartida-PI.md)**: Intellectual Property (Relating to inventions, methodologies, industrial processes, and machinery developed as a counterpart).
*   **[PU](../01_TECNOLOGIA_Institucional/TAK_memorial-sistema-construtivo-v7.md)**: Polyurethane (In the singular context of Institucional technology, it refers exhaustively to Vegetal Polyurethane / Bio-PU).
*   **[PVIT](../.agents/rules/GOV_regimento-pvit-audiovisual.md)**: Institucional Visual and Industrial Standard (Audiovisual Application and Auditing Regulations - Aesthetic Protocols).
*   **[SMGA](../02_PESQUISA_DESENVOLVIMENTO/TAK_nota-tecnica-smga.md)**: Environmental Monitoring and Management System (and/or Logistics Asset Management).
*   **[TAK](../01_TECNOLOGIA_Institucional/TAK_memorial-sistema-construtivo-v7.md)**: Indexing code/prefix for documents, engineering memorials, and proprietary assets of the Institucional nucleus.
