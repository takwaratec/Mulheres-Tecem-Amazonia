## 1. Object of the Memorandum

This memorandum summarizes the current state of the consolidated budget for the Women Who Weave the Forest Project and details...

## 3. Critical Pending Issues to be Addressed with UnB

### 3.1. Financial and Economic Counterpart
Define target counterpart range (10–20% of the total value) and value assets (IP, infrastructures, technical hours).

### 3.2. Economic-Financial Modeling
Calculation of NPV, IRR, and Payback for the business units (Biorefinery, Bioconstruction, Sanitation).

### 3.3. Carbon Certification
Formal decision on combination of standards (VERRA VM0044, Plan Vivo) and role definition.

### 3.4. Legal Formalization
Preparation of asset valuation reports and draft counterpart commitment letters.

## 4. Proposed Next Steps

1. **Alignment Workshop:** Review the counterpart package and carbon standards.
2. **Technical Deliverables (30–45 days):** Completed financial model and Acre market report.
3. **Dossier Consolidation (60–90 days):** Finalizing the budget and technical annexes.
