    Conceituação Técnica: Mini-Reator de Captura de Pirolenhoso

    1. Objetivo Técnico (Fase 1 e 2 de P&D)

    While the industrial line involves full gas capture for methanol synthesis, the Community Mini-Reactor focuses on the capture efficiency of Pyroligneous Extract (Wood Vinegar) and the direct combustion of non-condensable gases for thermal feedback.

    This device is the chemical heart of the Polo Takwara, allowing the community itself to produce the preservative agent necessary for bamboo treatment.

    2. Lógica de Funcionamento e Simplificação

    Different from the full cascade reactor, the Mini-Reactor operates in transition: 1. Biomass Pyrolysis: Bamboo residues are heated in a sub-oxygenated environment (retort). 2. Primary Condensation: Hot gases pass through a simplified condenser (coil in a water tank or cooled piping). 3. Pyroligneous Collection: The liquid (wood vinegar) is collected in HDPE drums. 4. Residual Gas Combustion: Non-condensed gases (syringol, guaiacol, and other combustible gases) are directed back to the base of the Rocket Stove, serving as thermal reinforcement ("clean burn").

    3. Diferenciais em Relação ao Reator Industrial

    • Absence of Methanol Cascading: There is no complex cracking and synthesis system, reducing the need for certified pressure vessels and high-precision electronic sensors.
    • Manual Feeding: The pyrolysis temperature is controlled by adjusting the air intake and biomass volume in the Rocket Stove.
    • Local Maintainability: All connections are made via simple flanges or joints sealed with clay/ash, facilitating the cleaning of persistent tar.

    4. Cenário Imagético

    A small set of vertical metal vessels (retorts) interconnected by copper or steel tubes dipping into cold water tanks. At the final outlet, an injector nozzle blows a bluish flame back into the stove, indicating the successful combustion of residual gases. At the foot of the system, polyethylene containers receive the dark, potent liquid (pyroligneous extract) that will be diluted to bathe the settlement's bamboo culms.
